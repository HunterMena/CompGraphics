Haunted Grocery Run

Objective: Collect all list items, avoid the monster, and reach the exit.

    Controls

    • WASD: Move
    • Space: Jump
    • Mouse: Look around
    • ← →: Turn (no mouse needed)
    • E: Pick up item
    • F: Toggle flashlight
    • R: Restart

    Press Start to begin. Click the game to re-enable mouse look.
